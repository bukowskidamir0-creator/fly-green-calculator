Methodology

What the model calculates

1. Distance

The entered distance is multiplied by 1.07 to account for routing, diversions, and approach patterns.

2. Aircraft

Each aircraft type has an estimated CO2 per kilometer, seat count, and an additional LTO block for landing and takeoff.

3. Passenger

Flight emissions are allocated across the entered number of passengers using passenger weight share.
